Nutrition Tips
Balanced Diet

Aim for a macronutrient ratio of approximately 45-65% carbs, 10-35% protein, and 20-35% fat for a balanced diet.

Fruits & Vegetables

Try to include at least 5 servings of fruits and vegetables in your daily diet for essential vitamins and minerals.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses (64 oz) of water daily. Proper hydration is essential for metabolism and overall health.
